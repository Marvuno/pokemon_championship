# Pokemon Championship
Are you sick of the stupid Pokemon AI? 
Do you feel that Pokemon battle has been too easy?

I present to you Pokemon Championship, a game to stimulate advanced Pokemon battle experience in Singleplayer.
Pokemon Championship introduces features including:

- 239 competitive Pokemon, including 39 NEW Custom Pokemon with UNIQUE typings (Fire/Ground, Normal/Ghost, Fire/Grass etc.)
- 382 moves, including 45 Custom moves with UNIQUE effects
- 150 abilities, including 12 Custom abilities
- Character Abilities
- Intelligent AI
- Full Demon Slayer themed Team (If you are a fan of it)

## Basic Info
Each attempt will be a 5-round Swiss format tournament. In other words, you will have to play 5 rounds
every time to determine the results. However, you are free to quit early. Each round, you have the option
to receive different Pokemon for your team, so you have the opportunity to use different Pokemon, and
develop strategies with them. Try using underwhelmed Pokemon to beat stronger opponents!

Aside from ranking, your result will be justified with a Rating system. If you perform well, you
get higher ratings and thus better Pokemon over the time.
More will be covered in the Tutorial inside the game.

## How to Play
Download the zip file from releases and extract it. Click on the main.exe. That's it!
However, there may be some unknown issues that render the game unplayable for some users. Please
do not hesitate to give me feedback if it happens.

Right now, it is probably a Windows-only game (maybe Linux too, haven't tried) as Apple may not support
.exe file. You may need a virtual box or do extra work on the source code to play it.

Last, this is currently a Command Prompt Game. I will try to make it a Web Game in the future
if possible. I am also welcome to any kind of collaboration and suggestions. Please feel free.
Thank you very much.

Enjoy!
