## Pokemon Championship
Are you sick of the stupid Pokemon AI? Do you feel that Pokemon battle has been too easy?
I present to you Pokemon Championship, a game to stimulate advanced Pokemon battle experience in Singleplayer. Pokemon Championship introduces features including:
- 239 Pokemon, including 39 Custom Pokemon with UNIQUE typings (Rock/Ghost, Normal/Ghost, Fire/Grass etc.)
- 382 moves, including 45 Custom moves with UNIQUE effects
- 150 abilities, including 12 Custom abilities
- Character Abilities
- Intelligent AI
- Full Demon Slayer themed Team (If you are a fan of it)
## Basic Info
Each attempt will be a 5-round Swiss format tournament. In other words, you will have to play 5 rounds every time to determine the results. However, you are free to quit early. Each round, you have the option to receive different Pokemon for your team, so you have the opportunity to use different Pokemon, and develop strategies with them. Try using underwhelmed Pokemon to beat stronger opponents!
Aside from ranking, your result will be justified with a Rating system. If you perform well, you get higher ratings and thus better Pokemon over the time. More will be covered in the Tutorial inside the game.
Moreover, to keep it original, the original Pokemon will only have original moves and abilities, unless otherwise specified in the changes.txt.
NOTE: This game is not meant to simulate competitive battle experience. While I have added most of the features inside, moves such as Substitute and Focus Punch and terrain is still missing. Moreover, EV, PP (replaced) and Nature (may add in the future) is not added yet.
Rather, you may treat it as a singleplayer Pokemon battle game that highlights replayability but has no story elements. You are only put into battles and must utilize the given Pokemon and their movesets/abilities and attempt to outplay your opponents (AI but improved). Some Pokemon is overpowered (especially the Boss tier) (mostly custom ones) but otherwise it is relatively balanced, since the original pokemons are all very original (same base stats etc.)
Last, you cannot choose particular moves for the Pokemon (or it will be too time-consuming). Rather, the Pokemon will get 4 moves out of a maximum of 6 moves randomly. You also have the option to keep the Pokemon you want after the game.
## How to Play
Download the zip file from releases and extract it. Click on the main.exe. That's it! However, there may be some unknown issues that render the game unplayable for some users. Please do not hesitate to give me feedback if it happens.
Right now, it is probably a Windows-only game (maybe Linux too, haven't tried) as Apple may not support .exe file. You may need a virtual box or do extra work on the source code to play it.
